Community Tagging Restrictions Information

Other users won't be able to add tags listed here to your submissions. In addition to blocking individual tags, you can forbid tag patterns by using * as a wildcard. Wildcard patterns may only use one * and must contain at least two additional characters. For example, sun* matches sunrise and sunset; *_pen matches fountain_pen and ballpoint_pen.

You can still add these tags to your own submissions.
